: About me

It's Lara, and i am a fashion designer specializing in women's clothing. My journey into fashion has been deeply personal and transformative. After experiencing a cerebral stroke and undergoing a challenging surgical procedure that resulted in double vision, I chose to channel this unique perspective into my designs. This condition led me to a creative breakthrough: I began designing a wardrobe where every piece incorporates a "double" aesthetic. My clothing features mirrored details and duplications that reflect my own visual experience.
