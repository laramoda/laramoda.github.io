: About me

It's Lara, and i am a fashion designer specializing in women's clothing.

My journey into fashion has been deeply personal and transformative. After experiencing a cerebral stroke and undergoing a challenging surgical procedure that resulted in double vision, I chose to channel this unique perspective into my designs.

This condition led me to a creative breakthrough: I began designing a wardrobe where every piece incorporates a "double" aesthetic. My clothing features mirrored details and duplications that reflect my own visual experience. This innovative approach has not only shaped my design methodology but has also become a hallmark of my personal style.

By turning a significant challenge into a creative strength, I've developed a distinctive approach to fashion that blends functionality with artistic expression. Each piece I create tells a story of resilience and transformation, offering a fresh perspective on timeless fashion.

Thank you for exploring my work, and I hope my designs inspire and resonate with you as much as they do with me.
