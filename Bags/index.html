:bags 2023-2025

Leather Handbag Designs

This selection of handbags was entirely designed and handcrafted by me, using locally sourced materials and created through the double methodology developed for my Diplopia Collection — the conceptual and design foundation of my final degree project.

This process explores duality and layered perception in design, a creative approach that I have since applied to new pieces and continue to refine today. Each bag embodies precision, structure, and narrative, merging craftsmanship with an experimental, personal vision of contemporary design.

collaboration with @archadeco

Summer Tote Design

This summer tote was designed and handcrafted with an integrated handle system entirely developed by us, seamlessly built into the structure of the bag itself. The piece combines functionality and elegance, reinterpreting traditional summer craftsmanship through innovative construction and contemporary aesthetics.

collaboration with Emma Tarrats

Maxxi Bag Project

The Maxxi Bag was entirely designed, conceptualized, and handcrafted by us — from its creation to the art direction of its photoshoot. This oversized fur bag features a transformable handle that can also be worn as a scarf, merging functionality, playfulness, and bold aesthetics.

Creative Direction & Shooting Design

As part of our creative process, we also specialize in designing and producing concept-based shootings — from the initial idea and moodboard to full artistic direction and execution. This particular project represents a capsule campaign built around a single piece, conceived to highlight its identity through visual storytelling.
